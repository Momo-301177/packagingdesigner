Form 09 — Carton
What a designer actually does

More than making it look good.

i

Shelf visibility

Engineering the pack to be seen and understood in a crowded aisle, in seconds, from a distance.

ii

Product clarity

Making it instantly obvious what the product is, who it is for, and why it is different.

iii

Brand positioning

Translating brand strategy into the look, language and feel a buyer trusts and remembers.

iv

Range architecture

A system that scales cleanly across every variant and SKU without losing the brand block.

v

Retail readiness

Compliance, mandatory information and print-ready artwork, correct before it reaches the press.
