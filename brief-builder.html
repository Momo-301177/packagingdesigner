What good looks like
01

Lead with the difference

One sharp sentence on why the product exists beats a page of features. It sets the whole design direction.

02

Name the shelf

Where it sells and who it sits next to tells a designer how loud, premium or plain the pack needs to be.

03

Brief everyone the same

Send the identical brief to two or three studios so you can compare their thinking, not their guesswork.
