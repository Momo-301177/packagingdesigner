    Who to hire

    Which kind of studio fits?

    Boutique studio

    1–5 people

    Hands-on senior craft, often with the founder on your project. The sweet spot for launching brands and first ranges.

    Medium studio

    5–10 people

    A dedicated account manager and finished artist. Smoother for multi-SKU ranges and steady delivery.

    Large studio

    10–20 people

    Runs large national accounts, usually with offices in more than one state.

    Global agency

    20+ people

    Manages household brands across the world, with offices in major business hubs.

    A skilled freelancer can suit a single, simple pack where you already have clear direction, but you trade away account management, finished art and a compliance backstop. The cheapest quote is rarely the true cost once rework and a brand that cannot scale are counted.
